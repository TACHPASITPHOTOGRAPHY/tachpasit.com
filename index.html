About me.

Photography is about four things: time, place, subject, and where you happen to be standing. Get those right — or wrong in the right way — and the picture exists. Everything else is secondary.

Sometimes the imperfect frame is the exactly right one. Not because imperfection is a style, but because the moment that arrived slightly ahead of the camera, or from the wrong angle, or before anyone was ready — that one often carries something the careful version doesn't. There's a peculiar kind of perfection in that.

The job, mostly, is to stay humble, stay low, and not get in the way of what's already happening. A wedding doesn't need directing. It needs someone paying close enough attention to be there when it does.

Based in Phuket since 2016. Available across Thailand and in Europe.
